About

I am the posterboy for journeys. Started in engineering, then moved to writing technical copy for a crypto company, and now settled in the world of creative copy and concepting. This was my internal journey, from the left part of my brain to the right.

Born in Kolkata, worked remotely for companies in Texas and Wyoming, and then moved to Berlin, and then Hamburg, and Milan, and Dusseldorf, and Amsterdam. This was my external journey.

This need to always keep moving, trying new things, and learning new things meant that I could only make a living out of being in advertising or as a pilot. And being scared of heights made that choice for me.

So I live vicariously through every new project thrown my way, learning about swiss dairy cows through one brief, and UK’s phonebooth graveyards through the next.

It’s fun, and keeps giving me an excuse to delay getting a pilot’s license. So I think I will stay in this ad world for a while.

If you think you have some projects that would keep my journey moving in interesting directions, please get in touch!
