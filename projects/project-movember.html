Movember

Bend Over Briefs

Thousands of men in the UK skip their prostate exams because they feel embarrassed about the most uncomfortable part of the test — bending over. This leads to many preventable deaths each year, which Movember, as champions of men’s health, cannot sit by and watch. So we launched a new clothing line that tells men it’s okay to bend over.
